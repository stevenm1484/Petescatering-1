About Us

Where skill meets individuality

Experience the perfect blend of timeless elegance and cutting-edge style at 1027 Hair Lounge. Our adept barbers seamlessly blend traditional precision with the latest techniques, while our innovative stylists bring your look to life with the hottest contemporary trends.

Our Work

Styles from the chair

Every service is personal — here's what we do best.

Braids & custom patterns

From box braids and knotless styles to intricate cornrows and feed-ins — our stylists are known for clean parts, sharp lines, and designs that last. Whether you want a classic look or something bold and creative, we shape every braid around your hair and your vision.

Locs & retwists

Starter locs, maintenance retwists, and loc styling — we keep your locs healthy and looking sharp. Our team understands the care locs need and delivers retwists and styling with precision parting and attention to detail.

Line-ups & kids cuts

Clean fades, crisp line-ups, and kid-friendly cuts in a welcoming chair. We make the experience comfortable for the whole family — sharp results whether it's a first haircut or a fresh weekly lineup.

The lounge experience

Step into a salon where diversity and expertise meet. 1027 Hair Lounge is a welcoming space on N Central Ave — a place where your individuality takes center stage and our skilled professionals work their magic to help you look and feel your best.

Fades & grooming

Whether you crave a refined classic cut or a daring, fashion-forward style, our barbers deliver personalized grooming that mirrors your taste. Precision fades, beard trims, and finishing touches — all in one visit.
