Food Service

All events include 2 main dishes and 4 sides (very full plate). You can also order more main or side dishes to accommodate your needs. All foods are cooked fresh with nothing frozen. Serving/warming dishes and serving utensils are included with full service.

Appetizers

I work alone so I try to keep things simple. Options include cheese & cracker trays, cold cuts trays (add $0.50 pp), chips & homemade salsa, cookies, fruit trays (seasonal), and veggie trays. All foods are fresh.

Drinks & Eating Utensils

These items are at an extra cost. I carry no china.

  • Drinks — water, lemonade, and coffee
  • Eating utensils — plates, spoons, forks, and cups (disposables)

FAQ

Questions & answers

Get to know Pete and how he works — in his own words.

What's your signature dish?

All my dishes are signature dishes. My tri-tip for the meat dishes and Southern-style green beans for vegetables are my signature dishes. You have caterers who cook a lot of different dishes and they are OK but not great. They should cook their favorite foods and the most popular dishes on their menus and make them taste great — so everything that caterer makes tastes great.

That's why I have a 5-star rating on Thumbtack. I'm new to Bark and have no reviews yet, but they will come soon. You can look me up on Thumbtack and read some great reviews from my customers that I'm very proud of.

How do you go about creating the perfect menu for your clients?

By letting the customer choose what they want — and from that, making it better than they expected it to be.

What do you love most about your job?

Meeting my customers and their families, traveling to parts unknown to see things I never knew were there.

What inspired you to start your own business?

I have always enjoyed the kitchen, and now that I have time, I would like to see others enjoy my food too.

Why should our clients choose you?
  • If they want something very tasty and fresh, then they want me.
  • If they want something that will tickle their taste buds, then they want me.
  • If they want good quality food, then they want me.
  • If they want great service, then they want me.
  • If they want a caterer who puts lots of TLC into the food they prepare, then they want me.
Can you provide your services online or remotely?

No. All services are in person, not online. The only thing you may see online is a picture of what was made.
