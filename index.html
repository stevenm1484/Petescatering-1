FAQ

Common questions

I've never had a website. Is this complicated for me?

Not at all — that's our specialty. You talk, we build. You never touch code, hosting, or settings. After launch, any change is one phone call away.

How long does it take?

24 hours or less. We build your complete website within a day of our first conversation — that's our promise.

What if I don't like the design?

We build the entire site for free before you pay anything. You review it, request changes, and only pay when it's exactly right. If it's not for you, you walk away owing nothing.

Do I own my website and domain?

Yes — 100%. The domain is registered in your name, and if you ever leave, everything goes with you.

Can you update my site after it launches?

Yes. Your monthly maintenance covers unlimited small edits — new photos, menu changes, hours, announcements — usually done same-day.

Does the Luxury package include ad spend?

No — and we want to be upfront about that. Your $1,000 setup covers the website, AI call answering, and us managing your Google, Facebook, and Instagram ad campaigns. The money that actually goes to Google and Meta for the ads themselves is separate, paid by you directly to those platforms. For most local businesses, we recommend budgeting about $1,000–$2,000 per month in total ad spend to see meaningful results.
